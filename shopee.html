Overview

I was a customer experience designer intern in Shopee, which is the leading e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia. My role was to redesign the voucher wallet. I was extremely lucky to have had the opportunity to join the team right before launch and to follow along the journey.

My timeline was 20 weeks long. I started off by gathering existing research and working with the user researcher to learn the best approach to conducting interviews. I also worked closely with the product team to learn about the constraints and better understand the platform. I explored and shared my solutions with the design team, but also presented to the larger team to receive feedback. My favorite part was developing the interactive prototype of my final solution.
