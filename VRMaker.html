Overview

At iStaging, we design accessible and intuitive immersive products for the real estate and architecture industries.

VR maker turns smartphones into a powerful VR camera, allowing users to easily create virtual tours of any space. It provides an innovative experience for real estate, especially for the ones who can't make it to the properties in person. Virtual tours help real estate agents and buyers have an efficient house viewing experience.

Reflection and Take away

In Taiwan, the average age of realtors is between 45-60. They are unfamiliar with VR products, and even afraid of technology. Our goal is to make VR Maker accessible and intuitive for everyone. With the enhanced user experience, Redfin, one of the biggest real estate companies in the U.S., chose to partner with our company for their photographic solution. Moreover, our PropTech innovations won the 1st Digital Singularity Awards in 2019 for transforming the real estate industry.
